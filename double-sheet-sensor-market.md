<p><ul><li>Unilateral Contact</li><li>No Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Double Sheet Sensor market is segmented into two primary types: unilateral contact and no contact. Unilateral contact sensors detect double sheets by making direct contact with the materials, offering reliable detection through pressure changes. In contrast, no contact sensors utilize electromagnetic or ultrasonic waves to identify double sheets without physical contact, ensuring minimal wear and tear and greater sensitivity. Both types serve critical roles in manufacturing and automation, enhancing safety and efficiency in material handling processes.</p></p>
