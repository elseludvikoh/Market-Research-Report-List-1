<p><strong>The Electronic Expansion Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Expansion Valves</li><li>Electric Expansion Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic expansion valves market includes two primary types: electromagnetic expansion valves and electric expansion valves. Electromagnetic expansion valves utilize solenoid-operated mechanisms to control refrigerant flow, optimizing efficiency and performance in HVAC systems. Conversely, electric expansion valves employ stepper or servo motors for precise control, allowing for more flexibility and quicker response times in variable load conditions. Both types enhance energy efficiency and system reliability, supporting advancements in modern refrigeration and air conditioning technologies.</p></p>
