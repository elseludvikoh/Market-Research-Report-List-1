<p><strong>The Automotive Brake Actuation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brake Booster</li><li>Tandem Master Cylinders (TMCs)</li><li>Brake Fluid Reservoir</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive brake actuation systems market includes various components essential for efficient braking performance. Brake boosters enhance the driver's force on the brake pedal, providing greater stopping power with less effort. Tandem master cylinders (TMCs) ensure balanced braking by distributing hydraulic pressure to multiple circuits, improving safety and responsiveness. Brake fluid reservoirs store the hydraulic fluid necessary for the braking system to operate, maintaining optimal fluid levels for effective braking action. Together, these components are vital for vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
