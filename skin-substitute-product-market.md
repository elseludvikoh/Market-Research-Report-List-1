<p><p>The skin substitute product market is categorized into two main types based on the source: animal-derived and human-derived. Human-derived substitutes, which may include cadaveric skin or cultured skin cells, provide a closer match in biochemical properties and are essential for more complex cases like extensive burns or chronic wounds, promoting better integration and healing.</p></p>
