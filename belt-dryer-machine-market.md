<p><p>The belt dryer machine market features various configurations, primarily categorized into single-stage and multi-stage belt dryers. Single-stage belt dryers operate using one conveyor belt, providing a straightforward drying process ideal for smaller production needs. In contrast, multi-stage belt dryers use multiple conveyor belts, allowing for more efficient and uniform drying by providing extended drying times and improved energy efficiency, suitable for larger volumes of materials.
