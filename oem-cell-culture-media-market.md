<p><p>The OEM cell culture media market encompasses two primary types: liquid cell culture media and dry powder cell culture media. Liquid cell culture media are ready-to-use solutions, providing essential nutrients and growth factors for cell proliferation. They are convenient for immediate application in research and production. In contrast, dry powder cell culture media are concentrated formulations that require rehydration before use. This type offers advantages in storage, stability, and customization, appealing to various applications in biotechnology and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
