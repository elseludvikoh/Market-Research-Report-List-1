<p><strong>The CT Diagnostic Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional CT scanners</li><li>Cone beam CT scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CT diagnostic imaging market encompasses two primary types: conventional CT scanners and cone beam CT (CBCT) scanners. Conventional CT scanners are widely used for detailed cross-sectional imaging, providing comprehensive information about internal structures, making them ideal for diagnosing various medical conditions. In contrast, cone beam CT scanners are specialized for dental and maxillofacial imaging, delivering high-resolution images with a lower radiation dose. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing diagnostic accuracy and facilitating effective treatment planning across diverse healthcare settings.</p></p>
