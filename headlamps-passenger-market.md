<p><p>Headlamps in passenger vehicles can be classified into several market types based on the lighting technology used. Halogen lamps are traditional and cost-effective, offering warm light but with limited efficiency. Xenon lights, or HID (High-Intensity Discharge) lamps, provide brighter illumination and better visibility, utilizing gas discharge technology. LED headlamps are energy-efficient, durable, and offer superior lighting with a longer lifespan. Other market types may include specialized or adaptive lighting systems that enhance safety and driving experience.</p></p>
