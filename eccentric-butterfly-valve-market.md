<p><p>The eccentric butterfly valve market is categorized into three main types: single eccentric, double eccentric, and triple eccentric valves. Single eccentric valves feature a shaft positioned off-center, allowing for improved sealing and reduced wear. Double eccentric valves have an additional offset that enhances flow characteristics and minimizes torque. Triple eccentric valves incorporate a third offset, which optimizes sealing performance and extends service life by reducing friction. Each type offers unique benefits suited for varying applications in industries such as oil and gas, water management, and manufacturing.</p></p>
