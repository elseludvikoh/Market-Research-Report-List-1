Horizontal type plastic granulators operate with a horizontal rotor, providing efficient material processing and easy maintenance, making them ideal for continuous operations. In contrast, vertical type plastic granulators feature a vertical orientation, optimizing space and enabling gravity-assisted material feeding. Both types serve crucial roles in recycling and manufacturing by converting plastic waste into granules for reuse, catering to various industry needs based on design and processing requirements.</p></p>
