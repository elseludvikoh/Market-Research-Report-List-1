<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic, Radiographic</li><li>Eddy Current</li><li>Magnetic particle</li><li>Acoustic Emission</li><li>Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) services market encompasses various techniques used to assess materials and components without causing damage. Ultrasonic testing utilizes high-frequency sound waves to detect flaws. Radiographic testing employs X-rays or gamma rays for imaging internal structures. Eddy current testing detects surface and near-surface defects using electromagnetic induction. Magnetic particle testing reveals surface cracks through magnetic fields. Acoustic emission monitors stress waves for defects, while terahertz imaging captures detailed images of materials' internal properties, enhancing inspection accuracy across industries.</p></p>
