<p><p>The metal processing lubricant market includes three main types: quenching, cutting and grinding, and corrosion preventives. Quenching lubricants are used to cool and solidify metals during heat treatment processes, enhancing hardness and strength. Cutting and grinding lubricants facilitate smooth and efficient machining operations, reducing friction and heat generation. Corrosion preventives protect metal surfaces from rust and degradation, extending the lifespan of machinery and components. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing metalworking processes and ensuring product quality.</p></p>
